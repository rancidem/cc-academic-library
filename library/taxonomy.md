## Tagging Rules

- Use lowercase only.
- Prefer hyphenated terms over spaces.
- Do not invent near-duplicate tags if a controlled term already exists.
- Use one tag from each relevant facet when possible.
- Keep tags specific enough to filter, but broad enough to reuse across entries.
