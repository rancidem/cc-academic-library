## Maintenance

- Refresh `sources/` when upstream repos change.
- Add entries for new command, agent, skill, and tool entry points as they are discovered.
- Keep the master catalog concise and route deep detail into the library pages.
- Track sync dates and notable changes in `notes/maintenance.md`.
